# Guitar Practice Analytics Platform

## Purpose

The Guitar Practice Analytics Platform is a system designed to measure, organize, and analyze the learning process of guitar students and professional musicians.

The project aims to apply principles commonly used in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics to the domain of musical practice.

The platform provides a structured approach for tracking:

* Technical exercises.
* Repertoire development.
* Practice quality.
* Tempo progression.
* Long-term goals.
* Student performance.

The system is intended to support different usage scenarios:

* Self-managed musicians.
* Professional guitarists.
* Teachers and private studios.
* Conservatories and educational institutions.

---

## Main Objectives

### Practice Measurement

Capture practice sessions and transform them into measurable data.

### Progress Tracking

Monitor the evolution of quality, tempo, repetitions, and technical development over time.

### Goal Management

Support short-term and long-term goals, including:

* Weekly goals.
* Monthly goals.
* Semester goals.
* Concert preparation.
* Competitions.
* Auditions.
* Exams.

### Business Intelligence

Provide dashboards and analytics that help identify:

* Improvement trends.
* Bottlenecks.
* Effective exercises.
* Student engagement.
* Teacher effectiveness.
