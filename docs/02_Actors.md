# Actors

## User

A person who interacts with the platform.

Depending on the context, a user may act as:

* Teacher
* Student
* Self-managed musician

A user may belong to an institution or operate independently.

---

## Institution

An educational organization that groups teachers and students.

Examples include:

* Conservatories
* Music schools
* Academies
* Universities

An institution may contain multiple teachers and multiple students.

---

## Teacher

A user responsible for organizing the learning process.

Teachers can:

* Create study plans.
* Assign exercises.
* Assign repertoire.
* Define goals.
* Define difficulty levels.
* Monitor student progress.

A teacher may also register personal practice sessions.

---

## Student

A user responsible for executing and recording practice sessions.

Students can:

* Register practice sessions.
* Track quality and tempo.
* Review progress.
* Follow study plans.

Students may belong to an institution or work independently.

---

## Self-Managed Musician

A musician who performs both teacher and student roles.

This actor creates personal study plans and records practice data without external supervision.
